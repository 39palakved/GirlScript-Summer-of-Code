Schedule For GSSOC'21

Project Assignment to Mentors 4th March - 5th March

The project assignment to the mentors will be finalized.

- GSSOC'2021
First Phase of Contribution Starts 8th March

This day marks the beginning of the 3-month journey. All the projects will be open during March. All the participants can contribute to the development of any of the projects.

- GSSOC'2021
The leaderboard will be open. 10th March

The leaderboard displays the points of every participant. It will be made visible to the community from this day onwards.

- GSSOC'2021
The Community Bonding sessions 10th March - 12th March

During the sessions, the participants will get the opportunity to bond with their teammates and mentors. We will also conduct several workshops to help the participants get started with their open source journey and also in building their profile.

- GSSOC'2021
Personalized allotment of projects to the participants 1st April

The participants will be permitted to join any one project of their choice. The organizing committee will take note of the preference of every participant and allot the most suitable project to everyone.

- GSSOC'2021
Coding period ends 31st May

This day marks the end of the 3-month journey.

- GSSOC'2021
Results will be declared. 2nd week of June

In the 2nd week of June, the results of GSSOC’21 will be declared.

- GSSOC'2021
